Who We Are!

KARAM is one of the leading manufacturers of Personal Protective Equipment in India. The vast range of PPE from KARAM is known for its finest quality and performance. We also provide the best & exclusively customized Industry specific fall protection safety solutions. KARAM Industries, Sitarganj spanning over an area of more than 20 acres caters to the Indian market. Our marketing team is vastly spread out in India with our sales and marketing offices situated in Delhi-NCR, Mumbai, Kolkata, Bangalore, Ahmedabad, Hyderabad, Pune, Chennai, etc. KARAM has a wide network of dealers and distributors to enable easy access and our products to end customers.
