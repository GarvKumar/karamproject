About Us

KARAM specializes in the field of Safety Harness & Body Belts, Hooks & Karabiners, Lanyards, Anchor Points, Horizontal Lifelines, Vertical Lifelines, Tripods & Winches, Rescue & Descent Equipment, Self Retracting Lifelines, Safety Helmets, Safety Shoes, Protective Eyewear, Hearing Protection, Welding Protection, Respirators etc. All these products undergo stringent quality checks and are certified as per IS & DGMS and other International norms. KARAM also offers customized Training and Consultancy to combat Safety Hazards in different industries in India.

Manufacturing Set-Up at KARAM

KARAM has a strong manufacturing set-up, equipped with the finest and latest technology driven machinery. All systems and processes are assessed by SGS UK, and registered to ISO 9001-2015.

LUCKNOW UNITS

Located in the outskirts of the city of Lucknow, about 500kms to the South-East of New Delhi, the Factory is spread over a span of more than 23 acres. A Unit where all the products are manufactured completely in-house is entirely vertically integrated, and hence is unique in its kind. All the components of the Fall Protection range of KARAM products are produced within one large campus, and yarn and steel are credited as being the only main raw materials for the same. The idea is to have complete control over the finest details of manufacturing to reach the highest levels of quality standards. The Metal operations involved are extensive, covering all aspects of manufacturing on CNC machines The Textile Operations involve starting from the basic raw material -high tenacity yarn, manufacturing the entire range of webbing, twisted rope and kernmantle rope. In addition to this, it also permits extreme levels of customization for the customers, in order to fulfill their needs.
